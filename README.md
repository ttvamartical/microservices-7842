# Microservices vs. Monolith Overview

This resource explores the key differences between monolithic and microservices architectures.
It explains when organizations should choose one model over the other based on business and technical requirements.
The guide covers architectural decision-making frameworks and migration planning strategies.
It highlights the importance of Domain-Driven Design for defining clear business boundaries.
Readers will learn how bounded contexts support scalable software systems.
The content also examines service-oriented thinking and modernization best practices.
Practical migration approaches such as incremental extraction and API-led transformation are discussed.
The article emphasizes balancing scalability, maintainability, and operational complexity.
It is designed for technology leaders, architects, developers, and digital transformation teams.
